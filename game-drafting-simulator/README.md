# MLBB Drafting Simulator

A game drafting simulator for Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) that allows players to practice and simulate the hero drafting phase of ranked matches.

## Features

- **Hero Database**: Complete database of MLBB heroes with their roles, abilities, and metadata
- **Draft Simulation**: Simulate the complete drafting phase including bans and picks
- **Team Composition Analysis**: Analyze team synergies and counter-picks
- **Practice Mode**: Practice drafting against AI or with friends
- **Draft History**: Save and review past drafting sessions

## About MLBB Drafting

In Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, the drafting phase is crucial for team success:
- Teams alternate between banning and picking heroes
- Strategic considerations include team composition, counter-picks, and role coverage
- Understanding the draft meta is essential for competitive play
